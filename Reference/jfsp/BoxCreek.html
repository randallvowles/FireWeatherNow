Here we have the - forecasts that were issued for the Box Creek Prescribed Burn between 14 May 2012 and 17 June 2012.
It occurred in the Fishlake National Forest in South Central Utah.

A test fire was performed on 15 May, followed by further burning starting on 29 May. On 4 June, it was discovered that a portion of the fire had not been extinguished, and under adverse conditions began to spread outside of the prescription. It was declared a wildfire and ended up burning a total of 1396 acres uncontrolled starting on 4 June.
