This version of the page contains all of the spot forecasts that are in the most basic sense parse-able. It still allows selection of all forecasts, by type, and by WFO. You can also adjust what month(s) you want to look at, as well as different "lead times", meaning the time between when the request was submitted and when the forecast was issued.

Search and Rescue forecasts are marked in green, Hazardous Materials are in blue, Prescribed burns are pink with fuchsia flames, and Wildfires are red with white flames.

A Note: Any selectable set marked as "LARGE!" (Western Region, for example) will slow down your browser - select at your own risk.
